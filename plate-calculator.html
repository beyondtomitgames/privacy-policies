Privacy Policy — Plate Calculator

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Plate Calculator ("the App") is developed and operated by Tomit Games. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information. All data you enter (weights, personal records) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location)
  • App interaction data

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App will ask for your consent before serving personalized ads, in accordance with GDPR. You may withdraw consent at any time through the App settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases

If you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, the transaction is processed by Google Play. Purchase status is managed by RevenueCat. Neither Tomit Games nor RevenueCat stores your payment details. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

All app data (personal records, settings) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: beyond.tomit.games@gmail.com
